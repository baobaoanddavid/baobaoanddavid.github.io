Travel + Accomodations

We’re excited to welcome you to Ithaca, NY — a charming town in the heart of the Finger Lakes region. Below is everything you need to know to make your journey and stay as smooth as possible for our special day.

Wedding Venue Locations

Ceremony: Sage Chapel at Cornell Campus

Nestled within the scenic Cornell campus, Sage Chapel offers a picturesque setting for our ceremony.

Reception: Large Pavilion in Stewart Park

Celebrate with us in the beautiful outdoor space of Stewart Park, perfect for a memorable evening under the stars. Getting Here

By Car:

Ithaca is easily accessible via major highways. If you’re driving, please note: Ceremony: There is free parking in Hoy Garage on Cornell Campus, a short walk from Sage Chapel.

Reception: Parking is available at Stewart Park. There are many free parking spots.

By Air: The nearest airport is Ithaca Tompkins International Airport (ITH), located about 15–20 minutes from the wedding venues.

Local Transit: For those preferring public transportation, local bus services and rideshare options are readily available to take you from your hotel to the wedding sites.

Accommodations: We’ve reserved a block of rooms at Hotel Ithaca for your convenience. Enjoy modern amenities and a central location, making it easy to get to both the ceremony and the reception.

Booking: Please mention the “[Your Wedding Name]” hotel block when reserving your room to receive our special rate. Detailed booking instructions and the reservation code are included in your invitation packet.

Location: Hotel Ithaca is located within a short drive of both Cornell Campus and Stewart Park, ensuring you’re close to all the action. Extra Tips Exploring Ithaca: Take some time to wander the vibrant downtown, sample local cuisine, or enjoy a walk along one of Ithaca’s scenic trails. Questions? If you need any assistance or additional travel tips, please don’t hesitate to reach out to our wedding coordinator. We look forward to celebrating with you in beautiful Ithaca!
